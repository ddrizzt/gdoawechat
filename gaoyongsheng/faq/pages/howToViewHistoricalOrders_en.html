View my order history

You can find your order history in the WeChat official account.

  1. Log in to your GoDaddy account.
  2. Click Account and select My Orders.
  3. You will see your order list with details.

You can also find your order history in GoDaddy's website.

  1. Log in to your GoDaddy account.
  2. Go to My Account and click Account Settings.
  3. In My Profile page, click Order History.
  4. You will see your order history with details.
