Find the expiration dates of my products

You can find all your product records with expiration dates in the WeChat official account.

  1. Log in to your GoDaddy account.
  2. Click Account and select My Renewal.
  3. You will see your product list and the expiration dates.

You can also find your product records in GoDaddy's website.

  1. Log in to your GoDaddy account.
  2. Go to My Account and click My Renewal.
  3. See your product list and the expiration dates.

If your product will expire in less than 90 days, please renew or upgrade in advance.
